About Us

We're just local muscle car fans who enjoy living in an area with so many classic car shows. Every year we hit several of the events at Carlisle, both shows in Hershey, in addition to PA Farm Show events and local business meetups for motorheads. This site is where we will publishing our photo albums for each event we attend once we get everything uploaded. If you would like spot us some free tickets or special access for your car show or related event to have your hot rods featured on our site, please send us a note on the Contact page.
